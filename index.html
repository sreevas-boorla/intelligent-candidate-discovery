  • Hands-on database operations with Milvus, Pinecone, Qdrant, Weaviate, or Elasticsearch.

Ideal-to-Have Skills

  • Fine-tuning experience (LoRA, PEFT).
  • Learning-to-rank systems (XGBoost, neural).
  • Prior hiring/marketplace product exposure.
